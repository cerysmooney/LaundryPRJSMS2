Meet the Funky Owners!

Simon and Minty

We are a recently graduated couple that understands the struggle of everyday life. From running low on dress shirts, to staying up way past midnight on an assignment, we know how stressful it can get. We created Washing on Sunshine to be a safe haven for those who are looking to find a getaway from those burdens, as well as do laundry and recharge if need be.

Our Laundry Services

Running low on clothes for a big interview? No problem!

Fully equipped with large washing machines and long rows of dryers, Washing on Sunshine has everything you need to be a little more prepared. Accompained with affordable prices, we make sure everyone can use our services.

Our Fully Functioning Cafe

Why not enjoy a snack while waiting for your washing?

Come take a seat in our cafe. With wonderful windows to view outside and plants scattered throughout, you're sure to feel relaxed when the world around you seems chaotic. We offer a range of coffees, teas, and nibbles for you to enjoy and further take away your stress. A menu will be at every table to showcase what we have avalible!
